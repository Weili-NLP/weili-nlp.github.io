Wei Li（李伟）

About Me:


I am currently a researcher at Baidu NLP Department, working on Natural Language Processing.
I received the Ph.D. from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in 2019.
My main research interests include: Text Summarization, Text Generation, and Multi-modal Pre-training and Generation.
Check out my Google Scholar, DBLP or email me.
